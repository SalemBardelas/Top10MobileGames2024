Top 10 Best Mobile Games for 2024

  1. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

    Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB)

    MLBB is a fast-paced MOBA with 5v5 matches, each featuring unique heroes. Key features include:

    • Fast-paced gameplay (10-15 minutes)
    • Ranking system with multiple tiers
    • Esports presence (MPL, M World Championship)
    • Regular updates with new content
  2. Call of Duty Mobile

    Call of Duty: Mobile

    Offering classic multiplayer modes, Battle Royale, and Zombies. Features include:

    • Cross-platform play
    • All Call of Duty games combined on one game
    • Tons of maps to play with
    • Seasonal updates and events
    • Esports tournaments (World Championship)
  3. PUBG MOBILE

    PUBG Mobile

    A battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last standing. Key features:

    • Multiple maps and modes
    • Experience realistic graphics and physics on this game
    • Solo, duo, and squad gameplay
    • Frequent updates and events
  4. Genshin Impact

    Genshin Impact

    Explore the world of Teyvat in this open-world RPG with elemental combat. Features:

    • Gacha system for characters and weapons
    • Single-player and co-op modes
    • Classic anime feel MMORPG with stunning graphics
    • Regular updates and new regions
  5. CarX Street

    CarX Street

    An open-world racing game with car customization. Features:

    • Realistic driving physics
    • Dynamic weather and city environment
    • Realistic graphics for a mobile game
    • Single-player and online multiplayer modes
  6. BloodStrike

    Bloodstrike

    A fast-paced battle royale with strategic combat and a variety of characters. Features:

    • Unique abilities for each character
    • Unique battleroyale experience because your characters have powers while using guns
    • Fast-action shooting and combat
    • Team-based tactical gameplay
  7. Asphalt Nitro 9

    Asphalt 9: Legends

    Arcade-style racing with real-world cars and intense races. Key features:

    • Stunning graphics and dynamic environments
    • Perform stunts and drifts
    • Fun arcade style and extremely thrilling racing game
    • Single-player and multiplayer modes
  8. Honor of Kings

    Honor of Kings

    A popular MOBA game with 5v5 matches and a variety of heroes. Features:

    • Fast-paced gameplay and strategic battles
    • Stunning graphics and smooth gameplay
    • Newly released MOBA game that'l thrill anyone who dares to try
    • Various modes and competitive experiences
  9. NBA 2K24 MY TEAM

    NBA 2K24 MyTEAM

    Build your dream team in this sports simulation game. Features:

    • Collect player cards and upgrade them
    • Online multiplayer and competitive tournaments
    • Customizable jerseys and arenas
    • Experience realistic NBA game even without a console or a PC
  10. Shadow Fight 3

    Shadow Fight 3

    A 2D fighting game with RPG elements. Features:

    • Martial arts combat and special moves
    • Character customization and upgrades
    • Unique shadow art style
    • Arcade style combat experience like Street Fighter
